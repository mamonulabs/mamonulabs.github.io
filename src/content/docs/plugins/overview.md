# ForestWare Plugins

A collection of probability-based and creative audio plugins.

## Prob Series

Probability-based effects that add controlled randomness to your music:

- **[ProbCreepyArp](/plugins/probcreepyarp/)** - Probabilistic arpeggiator with creepy, unpredictable patterns
- **[ProbDropoutAudio](/plugins/probdropoutaudio/)** - Random audio dropouts for glitchy textures  
- **[ProbDropoutMidi](/plugins/probdropoutmidi/)** - Random MIDI note dropouts
- **[ProbPitchMidi](/plugins/probpitchmidi/)** - Probabilistic pitch shifting for MIDI
- **[ProbStutter](/plugins/probstutter/)** - Random stuttering and glitch effects

## Texture & Space

- **[StaticPlethorium](/plugins/staticplethorium/)** - Noise and texture generation
- **[SyncRoboVerb](/plugins/syncroboverb/)** - Synchronized robotic reverb
